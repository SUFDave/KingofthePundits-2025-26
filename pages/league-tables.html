Week 11 has delivered the most dramatic twist of the season! Rob's magnificent Full House performance - his first of the campaign - catapults him from fourth place into a historic 3-way tie for first on 21 points! Dylan clings to a share of top spot despite managing just 1 point from Coventry's 3-1 demolition of Charlton, while Mike surges into the three-way tie with an excellent 3-point haul combining Lincoln's home win and Liverpool's 2-0 victory at West Ham. Davo slips to fourth on 19 points after Aston Villa's 1-0 victory over Wolves proved his only consolation in an otherwise barren weekend. With three players locked together at the top and just 2 points covering the entire division, this title race has never been more open!

Division 2: Ben's Lead Becomes a Chasm!

Ben extends his dominance with another clinical 3-point performance to reach an imposing 31 points - now a massive 7 points clear of the chasing pack! His Manchester City home pick and Sheffield Wednesday 2-3 Preston away prediction proved spot-on, though Liverpool's comfortable win at West Ham denied him a potential Full House. With an extraordinary 55% success rate (18/33 correct), Ben is running away with the Division Two title. Dave K and Alex are now locked in a battle for second place on 24 points apiece. Dave K endured a nightmare scoreless week as Crystal Palace fell 1-2 to Manchester United (not a home win), Chelsea drew 1-1 with Arsenal (not an away win), and Sunderland beat Bournemouth 3-2 (not a draw) - three incorrect predictions! Alex salvaged a single point from Lincoln's win but similarly struggled. Myles remains rooted to the bottom on 15 points after another blank week, now 16 points adrift of Ben in a concerning position.
