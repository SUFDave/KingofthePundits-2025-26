In-depth Analysis

Division 1: DOUBLE FULL HOUSE Carnage at the Top!

Week 12 has delivered one of the most extraordinary results in King of the Pundits history - TWO Full Houses in the same week! Dylan and Mike both achieved perfect 6-point weekends to surge into a tied lead on 27 points each. Dylan's masterclass saw Bradford's 2-0 home triumph over Reading, Bromley's 1-0 away victory at Accrington Stanley, and the crucial Wrexham 2-2 Watford draw all land perfectly. Mike matched him stride-for-stride with Sunderland's Tyne-Wear derby 1-0 win over Newcastle, Aston Villa's thrilling 3-2 away victory at West Ham, and the Brentford 1-1 Leeds stalemate all hitting the mark. Rob picked up a solid 3 points from Arsenal's 2-1 home win over Wolves and Bristol Rovers 0-3 Swindon away win to sit third on 24 points. However, Davo endured a nightmare week with ZERO points as all three picks failed - Walsall 1-1 Shrewsbury (not a home win), Plymouth 1-0 Rotherham (not an away win), and Leicester 3-1 Ipswich (not a draw). He now trails by 8 points and faces an uphill battle!

Division 2: Ben's Dominance Reaches New Heights!

Ben extends his already commanding lead with another excellent 4-point haul to reach a staggering 35 points - now 8 points clear of the chasing pack! His Coventry 1-0 Bristol City home pick and Brentford 1-1 Leeds draw proved spot-on, though Leicester's dominant 3-1 home victory denied his away win prediction. With an extraordinary 56% success rate, Ben is cementing his position as one of the greatest Division Two performers in competition history. Dave K climbs to 27 points with 3 points from Chelsea's 2-0 Everton home win and Villa's spectacular 3-2 victory at West Ham. Alex sits third on 25 points after managing just 1 point from Cardiff City's thrilling 4-3 home win over Doncaster - both Blackpool 2-2 Lincoln (not an away win) and Nottingham Forest 3-0 Tottenham (not a draw) failed. Myles remains bottom on 16 points after his Arsenal home pick was the only success from his three selections.

Week 12 Highlights

Week 12 will go down in history as the DOUBLE FULL HOUSE week - the first time two players have achieved maximum 6-point hauls in the same weekend! Dylan claims his first Full House of the season while Mike adds his second, demonstrating their tactical brilliance across all prediction categories. The weekend saw a remarkable total of 24 points distributed across both divisions, making it one of the highest-scoring weeks of the campaign. The Tyne-Wear derby delivered for multiple pickers - Sunderland's 1-0 victory rewarded Mike's home pick while denying Dave K's draw prediction. The 1-1 stalemate at Brentford proved particularly lucrative, with both Mike and Ben cashing in on the 3-point draw. Conversely, Davo's scoreless week represents a crushing blow to his title hopes, while Myles and Alex continue to struggle at the bottom of Division Two with just 1 point each.
