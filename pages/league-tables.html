The gap between first and fourth place has compressed dramatically, with Dylan moving up to third on 5 points and Mike slipping to fourth with 4 points after a scoreless Week 4.

Division 2: Ben Extends Dominance

Ben has strengthened his grip on Division Two with another excellent 4-point haul, bringing his total to an impressive 12 points. His 64% success rate remains the best across both divisions. The real story is Dave K's remarkable Week 4 performance, scoring a massive 6 points to leap into second place with 10 points total. Week 4 Highlights

Week 4 belonged to the draw specialists, with multiple 3-point predictions paying off handsomely. Rob and Dave K both secured valuable draw predictions, while Dave K's incredible 6-point week included successful picks across all three categories. The week saw several surprising results that caught many players off guard, particularly in the away win category where success rates were notably low.

Statistical Insights

After four weeks, clear patterns are emerging. Ben and Rob have established themselves as the most reliable predictors with success rates above 60%. Home win predictions continue to offer the best success rates, while away wins remain the most challenging category. The strategic importance of draw predictions is becoming increasingly apparent, with successful 3-point picks often determining weekly rankings. Davo's early season dominance is being challenged as the competition intensifies.
