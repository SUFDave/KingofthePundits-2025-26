About King of the Pundits

Competition Overview

  • King of the Pundits is a competitive football prediction league where eight skilled predictors battle across two divisions over a 30-week season.
  • Matches allowed consist of Premiership, Championship, League One & League Two (Friday to Monday games only).

How It Works

  • Points System: Participants earn points based on prediction difficulty - Home wins are worth 1 point, Away wins earn 2 points, and correctly predicting a Draw awards the maximum 3 points.
  • Weekly Format: Each week, players select three matches - one they predict will be a home win, one for an away win, and one they believe will end in a draw.
  • Players must pick their matches on time. If a player forgets to pick their match on their pick day, they will have to wait until all picks have been submitted before they can choose their picks.

Prize Structure

The competition operates on a £2 per player per week entry fee, with a total of £476.00 collected so far this season.

This fund is split equally between the Betting Fund and the End of Season Prize Fund.

  • Total Betting Fund: £238.00
  • Total End of Season Prize Fund: £238.00
  • Overall Champion: £57.12 (24% of prize fund)
  • Division 1 Winner: £57.12 (24% of prize fund)
  • Division 1 Runner-up: £33.32 (14% of prize fund)
  • Division 2 Winner: £57.12 (24% of prize fund)
  • Division 2 Runner-up: £33.32 (14% of prize fund)

Promotion & Relegation

  • The top 1 player from Division 2 will be promoted to Division 1 for next season.
  • The bottom 1 player from Division 1 will be relegated to Division 2 for next season.
