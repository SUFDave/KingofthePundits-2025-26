About King of the Pundits

Competition Overview

King of the Pundits is a competitive football prediction league where six skilled predictors battle across two divisions over a 30-week season. Each participant makes weekly predictions across multiple football leagues, earning points based on the accuracy and difficulty of their picks.

The competition spans matches from the Premier League, Championship, League One, and League Two, focusing exclusively on Friday through Monday fixtures to maintain competitive balance and ensure all participants have equal access to team news and information.

How It Works

Points System: Participants earn points based on prediction difficulty - Home wins are worth 1 point, Away wins earn 2 points, and correctly predicting a Draw awards the maximum 3 points.

Weekly Format: Each week, players select three matches - one they predict will be a home win, one for an away win, and one they believe will end in a draw. This format tests both analytical skills and risk management.

Picking Order: The selection order rotates weekly based on current league standings, with lower-placed players choosing their matches first. This system provides strategic balance and keeps the competition dynamic throughout the season.

Competition Structure

Division One: Currently features Davo, Mike, and Dylan competing for the top prizes and ultimate bragging rights.

Division Two: Ben, Dave K, Alex, and Myles battle for division supremacy and the coveted promotion spot to Division One.

Promotion & Relegation: At season's end, the top Division Two performer earns promotion to Division One, while the bottom Division One player faces relegation, ensuring competitive integrity across both divisions.

Prize Structure

The competition operates on a £2 per player per week entry fee, generating a total prize fund of £180 over the 30-week season. Prize distribution rewards both divisional success and overall excellence:

  • Overall Champion: 28% (£50.40)
  • Division 1 Winner: 28% (£50.40)
  • Division 1 Runner-up: 17% (£30.60)
  • Division 2 Winner: 17% (£30.60)
  • Division 2 Runner-up: 10% (£18.00)

An additional £180 is allocated to a betting fund, adding an extra layer of excitement to the weekly fixtures.

Rules & Regulations

Match Selection: Only Friday through Monday fixtures from the Premier League, Championship, League One, and League Two are eligible for selection.

Submission Deadlines: All picks must be submitted before the first selected match kicks off. Late submissions forfeit that week's participation.

Duplicate Selections: If multiple players select the same match, the later submission becomes void, emphasizing the importance of both timing and strategy in match selection.

Pick Categories: Each player must select exactly one match for each category (Home win, Away win, Draw) per week.

The competition has evolved into a sophisticated prediction league that combines statistical analysis, football knowledge, and strategic thinking. Each season brings new challenges as participants adapt their approaches and learn from previous campaigns.
