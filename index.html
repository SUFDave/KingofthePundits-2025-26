The Season At A Glance

Welcome to the 2025/26 season of King of the Pundits! With 30 action-packed weeks ahead, our competitors are locked in a fierce battle for bragging rights and the ultimate prize. Here's what you need to know:

  • The season lasts for 30 weeks, where eight pundits compete across two divisions in the ultimate test of football knowledge and prediction prowess.
  • The scoring system rewards bold predictions: Home Win = 1pt, Away Win = 2pts, Draw = 3pts. The rarest and most valuable outcome is correctly predicting a draw.
  • After four weeks of intense competition, we've seen explosive debuts, consistent performers, and standout weeks that have dramatically reshaped the standings in both divisions.
  • With £2 per player per week entry fees, the prize fund continues to grow. Total prize money is split equally between the Betting Fund and End of Season Prize Fund, with the top performers sharing the spoils.
  • Current form suggests this could be the most competitive season yet, with new players making immediate impacts and established champions fighting to maintain their positions under intense pressure.
