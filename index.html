Previous Week's Results: Week One 🗓️

What a start to the new season! Week one saw a classic King of the Pundits comeback, with Davo taking an early lead in Division 1 with a brilliant performance, correctly picking a Home Win, Away Win, and Draw to bag a maximum 6 points. It was a tough week for the rest of the division, with Dylan and Mike each getting one point, and Mevsi unfortunately starting the season with zero.

Over in Division 2, it's a tight race at the top with both Alex and Ben securing three points each. Alex correctly predicted a draw, while Ben got a home and away win correct. Unfortunately, Myles and Dave K will be looking to get on the board next week after starting the season with zero points.

Latest Results: Week Two 🗓️

Week Two delivered some exciting results! In Division 1, Davo added a crucial one point to his total with a correct Home pick, keeping his lead. Dylan also saw an improved week, earning two points from a correct Away pick, moving up the table. Mike secured a single point with a correct Home pick but missed out on his other two chances. Mevsi, unfortunately, remains at the bottom of the table without a pick.

In Division 2, the competition is heating up. Dave K made a significant move by earning a huge three points with a perfectly predicted draw, giving him his first points of the season. Myles also got on the board for the first time with a correct Home pick. Ben managed to secure another point, but he has fallen from the top spot. Meanwhile, Alex had a tough week, missing all three of his picks and earning zero points.

The Season At A Glance 🔭

  • ⚽ A Thrilling 30-Week Season.
  • ⚽ 7 Players Going Head-To-Head In Two Leagues, Division One And Division Two.
  • ⚽ Matches Consist of Premiership, Championship, League One & League Two (Friday To Monday Games Only).
  • ⚽ League Match Picks Points Allocation: Home Win Correct (1 Point), Away Win Correct (2 Points), Draw Correct (3 Points).
  • ⚽ Players Must Pick Their Matches On Time. If A Player Forgets To Pick Their Match On Their Pick Day, They Must Wait Until All Picks Have Been Submitted Before They Can Choose Their Picks.
  • ⚽ Total Entry Fees Collected: 7 Players Pay £2 Per Week X 30 Weeks = £420.00.
  • ⚽ The Total Monies Collected Will Be Split Into Two: A Betting Fund Allocation Of £210.00 And A Prize Fund Allocation Of £210.00.
  • ⚽ Prize Fund Allocations: Overall Champion (Most Points From Div 1 & 2) 24% = £50.40, Div 1 Winner 24% = £50.40, Div 1 Runner Up 14% = £29.40, Div 2 Winner 24% = £50.40, Div 2 Runner Up 14% = £29.40.
  • ⚽ Division One Players Are Davo, Mike, & Dylan.
  • ⚽ Division Two Players Are David K, Ben, Myles, & Alex.
  • ⚽ The Top Player From Division Two Will Be Promoted To Division One For Next Season.
  • ⚽ The Bottom Player From Division One Will Be Relegated To Division Two For Next Season.
