Meanwhile, Ben continues his relentless march in Division Two, extending his lead to an incredible 39 points with another masterclass display!

WEEK THIRTEEN RESULTS

Week Thirteen has delivered a seismic shift in the Division One title race as Mike explodes into sole possession of first place with a sensational 5-POINT MASTERCLASS! His clinical double-strike - nailing both Liverpool's thrilling 2-1 away victory at Tottenham and Newcastle's dramatic 2-2 draw with Chelsea - propels him to 32 POINTS and opens up a commanding 5-point gap at the summit. The king has been crowned, and he's wearing blue!

Mike's tactical brilliance shone through as Tottenham vs Liverpool became the weekend's pivotal clash. With Spurs leading through a Salah own goal, Liverpool roared back with goals from Salah (redemption!) and Szoboszlai to claim a crucial 2-1 victory at the Tottenham Hotspur Stadium. His Newcastle 2-2 Chelsea draw prediction proved equally inspired - a pulsating encounter saw the Blues fight back twice to earn a point, delivering Mike that precious 3-point draw bonus. Though his Bournemouth home pick fell (they drew 1-1 with Burnley), Mike's weekend haul was more than enough to break free from the chasing pack!

In a tale of contrasting fortunes, Dylan crashed to earth with a nightmare ZERO-POINT performance that could prove catastrophic for his title hopes! Preston's 1-1 draw with Norwich denied his home pick, Southampton's 1-1 stalemate with Coventry was not an away win, and Wycombe's 2-1 home victory over Bolton ruined his draw prediction. He drops to joint-second on 27 POINTS, now 5 points adrift and watching his title dreams slip away. The perfect Week 12 Full House seems like a distant memory!

Rob bounces back brilliantly with a solid 3-point performance to join Dylan on 27 POINTS in joint-second place! Ipswich's commanding 3-1 home demolition of Sheffield Wednesday secured his home pick, while MK Dons' ruthless 4-0 destruction of Harrogate away delivered his away win. However, Plymouth's devastating 5-1 thrashing of Doncaster meant his draw prediction missed - but 3 points keeps him very much in the hunt, just 5 behind the leader!

Davo produces a crucial 3-point recovery performance after last week's horror show! Manchester City's clinical 3-0 demolition of West Ham at the Etihad secured his home pick, and Arsenal's gritty 1-0 away victory at Everton (Gabriel's late header proving decisive) delivered his away win. Though Wolves' 0-2 home loss to Brentford denied his draw, Davo climbs to 22 POINTS, staying within touching distance despite sitting fourth. The race is far from over!

In Division Two, the coronation march continues! Ben extends his seemingly unstoppable dominance with another commanding 4-point haul to reach a staggering 39 POINTS - now an imposing 9 points clear of second place! His Man City 3-0 West Ham home pick and Newcastle 2-2 Chelsea draw both landed perfectly, maintaining his extraordinary 56% success rate and phenomenal 85% home accuracy. With a 9-point cushion and 17 weeks remaining, Ben is practically measuring the curtains for the Division Two champion's office!

Dave K climbs to 30 POINTS with a solid 3-point performance featuring Aston Villa's 2-1 home triumph over Manchester United and Arsenal's 1-0 away victory at Everton. However, Leeds' emphatic 4-1 demolition of Crystal Palace at Elland Road denied his draw prediction. Alex manages 2 points from MK Dons' 4-0 away rout at Harrogate to sit third on 27 POINTS. Myles languishes on 17 POINTS after Cambridge's 2-0 home win provided his only success - his struggles continue at the bottom of Division Two.

With 17 weeks remaining, Week 13 has transformed both title races! Division One now features Mike as the commanding leader with a 5-point advantage - can Dylan and Rob mount a comeback, or will Mike's momentum prove unstoppable? Meanwhile, Ben's 9-point cushion in Division Two looks increasingly unassailable as he marches towards glory. The coronation awaits!

THE SEASON AT A GLANCE

Welcome to the 2025/26 season of King of the Pundits! With 30 action-packed weeks ahead, our competitors are locked in a fierce battle for bragging rights and the ultimate prize.

The season lasts for 30 WEEKS, where eight pundits compete across two divisions in the ultimate test of football knowledge and prediction prowess.
The scoring system rewards bold predictions: Home Win = 1pt, Away Win = 2pts, Draw = 3pts. The rarest and most valuable outcome is correctly predicting a draw.
After thirteen weeks of intense competition, Mike has seized solo control of Division One with a commanding 5-POINT LEAD on 32 points, while Ben extends his Division Two dominance to a massive 9-POINT CUSHION on 39 points!
With £2 PER WEEK entry fees, the prize fund continues to grow. Total prize money is split equally between the Betting Fund and End of Season Prize Fund.
