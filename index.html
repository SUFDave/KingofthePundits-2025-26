WEEK TWELVE RESULTS

Week Twelve delivered one of the most extraordinary moments in King of the Pundits history - TWO FULL HOUSES in the same week! Dylan and Mike both achieved perfect 6-point performances to surge into a tied lead at the Division One summit. This unprecedented double maximum has never been seen before in competition history, and transforms the title race into a thrilling two-horse battle!

Dylan produced a tactical masterclass with his first Full House of the season, earning 6 POINTS from three perfect predictions. Bradford City's 2-0 home triumph over Reading secured his home pick, Bromley's superb 1-0 away victory at Accrington Stanley delivered his away win, and the crucial Wrexham 2-2 Watford draw completed his immaculate weekend. He rockets to 27 POINTS at the Division One summit!

Mike matched Dylan stride-for-stride with his SECOND Full House of the campaign - the only player with multiple perfect weeks this season! Sunderland's memorable 1-0 Tyne-Wear derby victory over Newcastle secured his home pick, Aston Villa's thrilling 3-2 away triumph at West Ham delivered his away win, and the Brentford 1-1 Leeds stalemate completed his masterpiece. He joins Dylan on 27 POINTS in a dramatic two-way tie!

Rob picked up a solid 3 points to maintain contact with the leaders on 24 POINTS. Arsenal's comfortable 2-1 home victory over Wolves secured his home pick, and Bristol Rovers' 0-3 defeat to Swindon delivered his away win. However, his Stockport draw prediction missed as Stevenage won 3-1 away, denying him a share of the top spot.

Davo endured a nightmare week with ZERO POINTS as all three predictions failed spectacularly! Walsall's 1-1 draw with Shrewsbury was not a home win, Plymouth's 1-0 home victory over Rotherham was not an away win, and Leicester's dominant 3-1 demolition of Ipswich was not a draw. He slips to 19 POINTS, now 8 points adrift of the leaders in a concerning fourth place.

In Division Two, Ben extends his commanding lead with another excellent 4-point performance to reach a staggering 35 POINTS - now 8 points clear of the chasing pack! Coventry's 1-0 home win over Bristol City and the Brentford 1-1 Leeds draw both hit the mark, though Leicester's 3-1 home victory denied his away prediction. With a phenomenal 56% success rate, Ben is cementing his status as Division Two champion-elect!

Dave K climbs to second place on 27 POINTS with 3 points from Chelsea's comfortable 2-0 home win over Everton and Aston Villa's spectacular 3-2 away victory at West Ham. Alex sits third on 25 POINTS after managing just 1 point from Cardiff City's thrilling 4-3 home win over Doncaster. Myles remains bottom on 16 POINTS after his Arsenal home pick proved his only success.

With 18 weeks remaining, the title races have crystallized! Division One features a genuine two-horse battle between Dylan and Mike, with Rob lurking just 3 points behind. Meanwhile, Ben's 8-point Division Two cushion looks increasingly unassailable as he marches towards his coronation!

THE SEASON AT A GLANCE

Welcome to the 2025/26 season of King of the Pundits! With 30 action-packed weeks ahead, our competitors are locked in a fierce battle for bragging rights and the ultimate prize.

The season lasts for 30 WEEKS, where eight pundits compete across two divisions in the ultimate test of football knowledge and prediction prowess.
The scoring system rewards bold predictions: Home Win = 1pt, Away Win = 2pts, Draw = 3pts. The rarest and most valuable outcome is correctly predicting a draw.
After twelve weeks of intense competition, we've witnessed historic moments including the first-ever DOUBLE FULL HOUSE week - Dylan and Mike both achieving perfect 6-point performances to share the Division One summit on 27 points!
With £2 PER WEEK entry fees, the prize fund continues to grow. Total prize money is split equally between the Betting Fund and End of Season Prize Fund.
Current form suggests this could be the most competitive season yet! Ben's phenomenal 56% success rate leads the competition with a commanding 8-point cushion in Division Two, while Division One's thrilling two-horse race between Dylan and Mike promises an unforgettable finish in the 18 weeks remaining!
