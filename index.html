SEASON 2025/26

WELCOME TO KING OF THE PUNDITS

Eight skilled competitors battle across two divisions in a 30-week war of wits, where every pick counts and pride is everything. After six electrifying weeks, Davo extends his Division One lead to 13 points with his immaculate home record still intact, while Dave K maintains his grip on Division Two with 16 points!

WEEK SIX RESULTS

Week Six delivered the most dramatic result in King of the Pundits history - an unprecedented 4-WAY TIE in Division One as Mike, Dylan, Rob, and Davo all scored exactly 3 points!
Alex emerged as the week's overall champion with a magnificent 4 POINTS in Division Two, combining Stevenage's home win with a crucial 3-point draw as Port Vale and Northampton finished goalless.
Davo maintains his Division One summit with 13 points, extending his perfect home record to an incredible 6/6 with Newcastle's 2-0 victory over Nottingham Forest.
Rob finally broke his away win drought with Swindon's victory at Newport, combining it with Cardiff's thrilling 4-3 home win to reach double figures on 10 POINTS.
Dylan climbs to 11 points after finally breaking his draw duck! Derby's 1-1 stalemate with Southampton earned him the elusive 3 POINTS he'd been chasing for six weeks.
Mike showed dangerous form with both home and away picks hitting the mark - Arsenal's 2-0 win over West Ham and Manchester City's away victory at Brentford.
Myles picked up 3 valuable points including his first away win of the season as Tottenham triumphed at Leeds. He sits on 14 POINTS, just 2 behind the Division Two leaders.
Division Two remains extraordinarily tight with just 2 POINTS separating first from fourth place as the title race intensifies!

THE SEASON AT A GLANCE

Welcome to the 2025/26 season of King of the Pundits! With 30 action-packed weeks ahead, our competitors are locked in a fierce battle for bragging rights and the ultimate prize.

The season lasts for 30 WEEKS, where eight pundits compete across two divisions in the ultimate test of football knowledge and prediction prowess.
The scoring system rewards bold predictions: Home Win = 1pt, Away Win = 2pts, Draw = 3pts. The rarest and most valuable outcome is correctly predicting a draw.
After six weeks of intense competition, we've witnessed historic moments including the first-ever 4-WAY TIE in Division One, while Division Two has become extraordinarily tight with just 2 points separating first from fourth place.
With £2 PER WEEK entry fees, the prize fund continues to grow. Total prize money is split equally between the Betting Fund and End of Season Prize Fund.
Current form suggests this could be the most competitive season yet! Davo's perfect 6/6 HOME RECORD stands as the season's most remarkable achievement so far!
