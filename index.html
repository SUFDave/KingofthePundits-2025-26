SEASON 2025/26

WELCOME TO KING OF THE PUNDITS

Eight skilled competitors battle across two divisions in a 30-week war of wits, where every pick counts and pride is everything. After seven dramatic weeks, Davo maintains his Division One lead with 15 points, while Dave K extends his Division Two dominance to an impressive 21 points following his sensational 5-point Week 7 masterclass!

WEEK SEVEN RESULTS

Week Seven delivered absolute fireworks as Dave K produced a commanding 5-POINT PERFORMANCE to extend his Division Two lead to a commanding 21 points! His masterful draw prediction on the Crystal Palace vs Bournemouth thriller (3-3) secured him the maximum 3 points and cemented his status as the division's draw specialist.
In Division One, Dylan claimed the week's crown with 3 crucial points, correctly calling both Coventry's home victory over Blackburn and Arsenal's away triumph at Fulham. This sensational form sees him sitting just 1 point behind leader Davo!
Ben matched Dylan's excellence with 3 points of his own in Division Two, showcasing identical predictions on the Coventry and Arsenal fixtures. His consistency keeps him firmly in second place with 18 points, just 3 points off the summit!
The weekend's biggest shock came at Anfield where Manchester United stunned Liverpool 2-1 with a late Harry Maguire header - their first victory at Anfield in almost a decade! This upset cost Dave K his home win prediction but couldn't derail his magnificent week.
Davo's home fortress finally fell! After six consecutive weeks of perfect home predictions, his Bradford City pick ended in a 2-2 draw against Barnsley. Despite this, his away win on Chelsea (0-3 at Nottingham Forest) secured 2 points and maintained his Division One leadership.
Both divisions remain extraordinarily tight! Division One sees just 6 POINTS separating first from fourth, while Division Two features a thrilling battle with only 6 POINTS between the top four as the race intensifies!
